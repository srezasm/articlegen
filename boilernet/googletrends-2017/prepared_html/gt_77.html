Who is Jordyn Woods? Kylie Jenner collaborates with best friend on new Kylie Cosmetics collection – All the details on Jordyn from her modelling career to her other famous pals

JORDYN Woods has teased she is collaborating with best friend Kylie Jenner on a Kylie Cosmetics collection, but who is she?

By Maisie Bovingdon
Wednesday, August 29, 2018

Jordyn Woods is a model and best friends with Kardashian/Jenner family [WireImage]

Jordyn Woods is best known for being the best friend of Kylie Jenner – who recently gave birth to her first child with boyfriend Travis Scott – and it is safe to say the pair have been inseparable, as the 20 year old has appeared on various episodes of Keeping Up With The Kardashians , as well as her pal's spin-off show Life of Kylie.

But the star has proven she is more than just close friends with the reality star, as she has teased she is collaborating with Kylie on a new Kylie Cosmetics collection.

Jordyn has forged a successful career in the fashion industry away from her famous friends, and has even more exciting ventures in the future.

Here is everything you need to know about Jordyn Woods, from her celebrity pals, her career and rumoured romances...

Jordyn Woods has modelled for a string of fashion houses [Getty]

Who is Jordyn Woods?

Jordyn is a 20-year-old model.

She was born to Elizabeth Woods and father John Woods, who tragically passed away from cancer last year .

Similar to Kylie Jenner, Jordyn’s mother is her manager and the Talent/Brand Manager at Mixed Image Media and Woods Marketing group, as well as a photographer.

Jordyn’s sister is a musician who goes under the stage name JWoodzYX, while her brother is a tattoo artist and goes under the title jwoodzart on social media.

Model Jordyn Woods is best pals with Kylie Jenner [WireImage]

Jordyn Woods' modelling career

The 20 year old is a model, and was scouted on social media .

The star signed a contract with Wilhelmina modelling agency.

Jordyn made her debut catwalk show appearance two years ago in May 2016 when she modelled Christian Siriano’s collection for Lane Bryant.

The fashion icon has also collaborated with Addition Elle, appeared in campaigns with Ashley Graham, and modelled for ASOS.

In July 2016, Jordyn designed her first collection with Boohoo.com, and she has teased she is collaborating with Kylie Jenner on a Kylie Cosmetics collection, as well as her own label Secndntre.

Kylie Jenner and Jordyn Woods are best friends [Instagram]

Jordyn Woods' best friend is Kylie Jenner

Jordyn is best known for being longterm friends with Kylie, as well as the 21-year-old reality star’s family, as she attended Kim Kardashian West and Kanye West 's wedding in Italy in 2015.

The model has proved to be a huge support to Kylie who has recently given birth to her first child, as she kept the news a secret for the entire nine months.

It was hinted Jordyn was one of the first people to find out about Kylie’s pregnancy, as the Kylie Cosmetics mogul revealed in the To Our Daughter YouTube video she showed Jordyn and another friend her positive pregnancy test.

But Kylie has previously proved to be a great friend to Jordyn and donated a huge sum of money to her pal's family following the loss of her dad.

Jordyn has also modelled for Good American, a brand which Kylie’s pregnant half-sister Khloe Kardashian – who is expecting her first baby with boyfriend Tristan Thompson – has partnered with.

Jordyn Woods calls Will Smith her uncle [Instagram]

Who are Jordyn Woods’ other celebrity pals?

Jordyn boasts a close relationship with actor Will Smith, and his family.

The star has previously hailed the Hitch star her “uncle” in a sweet throwback photo, although the pair are not related.

The star previously set tongues wagging she was romancing Will’s son Jaden, 19, as they regularly share images together, and were spotted out and about together.

However, the pair have silenced rumours and insist they are nothing but great friends.
