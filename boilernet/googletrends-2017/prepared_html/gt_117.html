Money, Money, Money: Meryl Streep looks set to make £11m profit as her luxury New York penthouse is listed for sale

Meryl Streep and her husband Don Gummer are selling their four-bedroom penthouse flat in Tribeca, New York City, after 12 happy years there.

After 12 happy years Meryl Streep is selling her New York City penthouse for £19 million.

The Mamma Mia! star bought the four-bedroom flat in Tribeca for £7.8 million.

As befits a superstar, the exclusive home has a skylit entrance gallery and a wraparound terrace with views over Manhattan .

The muted interiors offset the statement art collection that belongs to Streep and her sculptor husband, Don Gummer. The art goes when they do.
