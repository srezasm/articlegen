Kylie Jenner and Jordyn Woods Party With Gold Cake Pops, Personalized T-Shirts, and a Ton of Pink Balloons

By Ariana Yaptangco
Sep 22, 2018
Getty Images

You know Kylie Jenner was going all out to celebrate her best friend Jordyn Woods' 21st birthday, especially after Jenner's own 21st birthday blowout this summer.

The besties kicked things off this past Thursday with a dinner in West Hollywood, wearing eye-popping mini dresses. Jordyn looked incredible in a sparkling orange number, while Kylie debuted pink hair in a $795 Alejandro Collection design.

There was even an adorable appearance from baby Stormi.
